## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Do:
- Use active voice ("We'll send your disputes" not "Your disputes will be sent")
- Be direct and clear ("Upload your report" not "Please consider uploading your report")
- Use metaphor strategically (warrior, armor, battle, quest)
- Empower the user ("You have the right to..." "You deserve...")
- Be specific about timelines ("30 days" not "soon")
- Use second person ("Your credit" not "A customer's credit")

### Don't:
- Use legal jargon without explanation
- Make promises you can't keep ("Guaranteed credit improvement")
- Use fear tactics ("Your credit is ruined")
- Be condescending ("Don't worry, we'll handle it")
- Bury important information in small text
- Use corporate speak ("Optimize your financial profile")

### Tone Across Sections:
- **Landing:** Bold, empowering, cinematic
- **Intake:** Welcoming, attentive, professional
- **Upload:** Clear, instructive, encouraging
- **Review:** Confident, detailed, authoritative
- **Payment:** Transparent, reassuring, direct
- **Confirmation:** Celebratory, forward-looking, supportive
